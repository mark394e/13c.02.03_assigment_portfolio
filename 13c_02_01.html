Fonts and UI Components

In the first assignment we were tasked with finding a font pairing, choosing a font hierachy and styling the cards according to best practices. I chose Josefin Sans for my titles and Lato as my body text. This was a pairing I found using Figma's Google Font Pairings. Regarding the cards, I refrained from using borders, rounded corners and shadows as this is not best practice. I placed the media/image at the top with CTA buttons below. I gave the buttons different colors to differentiate between them. You can see my solution here.
